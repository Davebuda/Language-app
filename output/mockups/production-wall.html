Same component, three lenses. One <ProductionWall level={...} /> renders for every user. The brick grid, the moment-of-contribution landing animation, the daily→weekly stacking, and the honesty legend are identical. Only the objective header, the hero count (foundations rørt / setninger produsert / lange setninger — never a %), and the weekly roll-up label change per level. Brick weight is encoded by fill + texture, never color alone (WCAG AA). B2 shows an honest interim — no fake lexical meter until that engine track exists.
