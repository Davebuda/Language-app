How the quiz feeds the engine: Q1 (experience) sets the starting concept pool (A1 only for beginners). Q2 (weakness) pre-weights certain error tags in the fingerprint, so remediation exercises appear earlier. Q3 (goal) influences sentence scenarios used in exercises (job/formal vs casual). The engine adapts from turn 1 — no wasted first session.
