Design decisions baked in

Dark landing, warm cream screensLanding stays #0d0d14 (matches your image). Dashboard/session/completion use warm #faf7f0.
Orange primary accentAll CTAs, progress bars, streak display. Warm, not purple (per CLAUDE.md ban).
Green for success/completionCompletion card background, mastered concept nodes, correct answer feedback.
Repair loop = warm amber tintInline below exercise. Mode shift is visible but not alarming. Never a red flash of failure.
Concept nodes = colour-coded by statusEach concept gets a distinct hue. Grey + lock for prerequisites not yet unlocked.
Guest mode — no login requiredUses a guest name ("Anna" placeholder). Banner at top offers sign-in to sync. All engine features fully functional.
